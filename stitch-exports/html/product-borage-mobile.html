Dried Borage Flower

$24.00 / 50g

"Corago, ego cor addo" — I, borage, bring courage.

A celestial blue blossom harvested at the peak of the lunar cycle. Known as the 'starflower,' these hand-dried petals offer a cooling, cucumber-like essence to rituals and elixirs. Historically used to lift the spirit and embolden the heart.

Sourced from a regenerative sanctuary in the High Atlas Mountains. Harvested at dawn when the dew is still heavy, preserving the essential mucilage and star-like form. Each batch is air-dried in a zero-light environment to maintain its deep cerulean hue.

  • • 3.5g of blossom per 250ml vessel.
  • • 85°C (185°F) spring water.
  • • Steep for 5 minutes in silence.
  • • Decant into glass to observe the color shift.

Rich in gamma-linolenic acid (GLA). Traditional uses include soothing inflammatory responses, supporting adrenal health during times of stress, and promoting a sense of tranquil confidence.
